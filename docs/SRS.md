# Momentum AI

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Author:** Gaurav Kaushik

---

# Project Overview

Momentum AI is an AI-powered executive productivity companion that helps users plan, prioritize, schedule, and complete their work before deadlines are missed.

Unlike traditional to-do applications, Momentum AI actively assists users by breaking down goals into manageable tasks, intelligently prioritizing work, generating optimized schedules, and providing context-aware recommendations using Google's Gemini AI.

---

# Problem Statement

People often miss deadlines because existing productivity applications only remind users instead of helping them complete their work.

Momentum AI aims to solve this problem by becoming an intelligent productivity assistant capable of reasoning about the user's workload and recommending the best course of action.
